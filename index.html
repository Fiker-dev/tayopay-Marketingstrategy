The story (quick, digestible)

Skim this like a founder memo. Each block is a decision.

1) What we’re solving

People choose remittance apps based on trust and certainty: fees, delivery time, and “will it work for my corridor?”

2) The wedge

Win a few high-volume corridors with hyper-local creative + landing flows. Then expand corridor-by-corridor.

3) The growth engine

Performance + creator distribution. A/B test creatives weekly, optimize onboarding daily, measure repeat send.

4) The 90-day sprint

Weeks 1–2 foundation, 3–6 scale, 7–12 compound: referrals, retention, and corridor ROI dashboards.
